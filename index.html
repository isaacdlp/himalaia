Custom Art

Let's design the perfect gift together

1) Tell me what you like

Imagination has no boundaries

The creations in this site can be just an inspiration to let your ideas fly. Write me a note with the ideas that inspire you and we will chat to make this piece unique.

2) I send you a proposal

Measure twice, cut once

Using your ideas as a starting point, I will send you a draft proposal that includes a detailed drawing of the piece as well as the time and cost involved in the process.

3) We agree on a final design

Keep iterating until it is perfect

We continue our conversation and incorporate any feedback you may have. You don't pay anything until you are fully satisfied with the proposed design.

4) I create the artwork for you

This is where the fun begins

I will then create the artwork, send you pictures once it is completed (in case you want any last minute changes or additions) and deliver it to your home.

And the most important of all... ¡A UNIQUE PIECE OF ART FOR LIFE!
